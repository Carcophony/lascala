How to Play
LASCALA DAILY · ONE GAME · ONE DAY

The Game

Every day, the same dice sequence plays for everyone in the world. A shooter climbs a numbered ladder. You predict what happens before each throw is revealed. No gambling. No money. Just you vs the ladder.

The Ladder

Numbers count down from the top. Top = 0 steps to go. The shooter moves by the difference between two dice — not the total. Roll a 5 and a 2? Move 3 steps. Roll two 4s? Double — game over instantly.

Your Predictions

Before each throw is revealed, pick one or more:

MAKE IT — shooter will reach 0 and win the game.
DON'T MAKE IT — shooter will roll a double and fail.
DIFF 0–5 — predict the exact difference on this throw.

Scoring

Correct game prediction: +100 pts
Correct diff prediction: +50 pts (bonus for rare diffs)
Hedge executed: +200 pts (backed both sides, both could win)
Wrong: 0 pts (no penalty — just the shame)
